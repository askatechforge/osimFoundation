PROFILE

OSIM FOUNDATION was formed in 2018 aiming at improving maternal and child health, sanitary pad project, implementing nutritional program, women empowerment in far unreached communities of Arusha region in Tanzania through health education and promoting health seeking behavior for communities living in marginalized areas.
